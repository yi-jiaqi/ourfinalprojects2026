Guide

Ever wanted to find a mooshroom island on your minecraft server but died or something came up before you had the chance to enjoy it? Well this Mushroom Island Sim is perfect for thoes people who love to explore! In this game you’ll have to travel across the island clearing all the poisonous glowshrooms from polluting your island. Visit villages, encounter rare jewels in caves and craft tools in this game! When you spawn in you will encounter a villager with a quest. This game took a lot of modifications and still probably could be changed! Hope you enjoy it and you can reach me at janeg14@nycstudents.net with advice or requests! Thanks! -Aria
